🚨 Warriors Expected to Sign Seth Curry, Reuniting Him with Stephen Curry

September 23, 2025 • Basketball News

The Golden State Warriors are making headlines once again. According to NBA insider Marc Stein, the team is expected to sign free agent guard Seth Curry, potentially reuniting him with his brother Stephen Curry for the first time in their NBA careers. 🤯 ...

A Family Reunion on the Court

While the Curry brothers have faced off as opponents many times, they have never shared the same uniform in the league. If finalized, this move would mark a historic moment for the NBA and a dream scenario for Warriors fans. Seth Curry has built a strong career of his own as one of the league’s most efficient shooters. With a career 43% shooting mark from three-point range, he ranks among the best in NBA history. Pairing him with Steph — the greatest shooter of all time — would instantly create one of the most dangerous perimeter duos in basketball.

Warriors’ Busy Offseason

The Warriors have been aggressive this offseason, already adding Al Horford, De’Anthony Melton, and Gary Payton II to their roster. The potential signing of Seth Curry further emphasizes the franchise’s determination to maximize Stephen Curry’s championship window as he enters the later stages of his career. Al Horford adds veteran leadership and frontcourt defense. De’Anthony Melton brings backcourt versatility. Gary Payton II provides elite perimeter defense and familiarity with the Warriors’ system. Adding Seth Curry to this mix not only strengthens the Warriors’ shooting depth but also adds a unique family storyline that will generate massive fan interest and media attention.

What This Means for Golden State

Golden State’s identity has always revolved around shooting, spacing, and unselfish ball movement. Seth’s skill set aligns perfectly with that system. Beyond the X’s and O’s, the chemistry between the Curry brothers could spark new energy inside the locker room and across Dub Nation. If the signing becomes official, expect the Chase Center to erupt as the Curry brothers light up defenses together. This move may not just be a roster upgrade — it could be the emotional spark the Warriors need for another deep playoff run.

Final Thoughts

The Warriors are sending a clear message: they are not done chasing championships. With Steph, Draymond Green, and potentially Klay Thompson still in the mix, adding Seth Curry could help fuel one last title push. Whether it’s for basketball strategy or simply the spectacle of seeing the Curry brothers share the floor, this is one move that has the entire NBA buzzing.

LeBron James Reportedly Wants to Play with Both Sons, Bronny and Bryce
September 23, 2025 • Basketball News

Former Los Angeles Lakers teammate Dwight Howard claimed that LeBron James has expressed a desire to bring Bryce into the Lakers’ system to eventually play alongside his family. ...

A Family Dream

Recent media speculation also suggests whispers that LeBron is aiming to share the court with both of his sons — Bronny and Bryce. 🫣 If true, this would be an unprecedented moment in NBA history, as no player has ever suited up alongside two of his own children in the league.

What This Could Mean for the Lakers

The Lakers have always been a franchise built on star power, and LeBron’s potential family storyline could bring a whole new wave of excitement. Bronny is already taking steps into his rookie year, while Bryce — still in development — could follow the same path in the coming years. Having LeBron, Bronny, and Bryce on the same roster would not only be a massive marketing phenomenon but also a basketball milestone that captures the imagination of fans worldwide.

Final Thoughts

While nothing has been finalized, the idea of LeBron creating the ultimate family legacy on the NBA stage continues to gain traction. Whether it happens or not, the conversation alone shows the influence and longevity of LeBron’s career.
